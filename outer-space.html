Astronomy

What is Astronomy?

Astronomy is the study of everything in the universe beyond Earth’s atmosphere. That includes objects we can see with our naked eyes, like the Sun, the Moon, the planets, and the stars. It also includes objects we can only see with telescopes or other instruments, like faraway galaxies and tiny particles. And it even includes questions about things we can't see at all, like dark matter and dark energy.

Exhibits

In addition to the "dome", we have multiple exhibits of the discoveries of astronomers since many hundred years ago and up until today. Come see videoes, pictures and read wonderful stories of how humans have dared to discover the world outside of our own athmosphere. We even have gigantic telescopes for you to have a peak at the universe beyond the world as we know it.
